Silent Hill Archive

Silent Hill is one of the most iconic horror game series of all time. It is known for its psychological horror, thick fog, disturbing monsters, symbolic storytelling, and deeply unsettling atmosphere. Rather than relying only on action or jump scares, the series creates fear through isolation, emotion, and mystery.

This fictional website is a tribute to the series and presents a short overview of some of its most important games. The site is designed as an educational HTML and CSS project inspired by a provided three-page layout.
