Featured Games

The Silent Hill series includes many memorable titles, but a few stand out as especially important. These games helped define psychological horror in video games and are remembered for their atmosphere, sound design, symbolism, and unforgettable visual identity.

Silent Hill

1999

The original game introduced players to the cursed town of Silent Hill. It became famous for its fog-covered streets, eerie music, radio static, and oppressive atmosphere. It laid the foundation for the entire series.

Silent Hill 2

2001

Silent Hill 2 is often considered the masterpiece of the franchise, and personnaly my favorite. It focuses heavily on grief, guilt, trauma, and psychological symbolism. Its story and atmosphere made it one of the most respected horror games ever made.

Silent Hill 3

2003

Silent Hill 3 continues the series with stronger visuals, disturbing enemy design, and a memorable main character. It blends psychological horror with intense environments and remains one of the most visually striking entries.
